Hi! I’m Lauren Moran, a 26 year old FIT graduate with a BFA in Illustration currently living in Manhattan, NY.

By day, I flex my design and illustration skills as a men’s graphic shirt designer for the company Freeze where I’m lucky to work with fun licenses such as Marvel, Transformers, WWE, and Star Trek. My designs have been sold to stores like Hot Topic, Target, ThinkGeek.com, and more.

By night, I’m a freelance illustrator always working with different mediums including oil painting, ink, watercolor, and photoshop. My personal work reflects my passion for pop culture, comics, and a general love of creating portraits of my favorite people.

I’m is always open and looking for new a fun projects, whether it be a spot illustration, shirt design, a personal portrait, or a comic cover. I’d also love try new things, so shoot me an email and lets start something fun!
